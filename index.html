From Omaha, For Omaha.

We're not a faceless corporation. We're your neighbors, building something we believe our city deserves.

The "Why"

The Counter Collective started over coffee in the Old Market. We saw the passion and dedication of local organizers, but also the struggle: outdated software, scattered spreadsheets, and too much time spent on admin instead of the mission. We analyzed the market and saw a clear gap for a 'logistics-first' platform designed for the operational realities of community work, not just fundraising.

Our Philosophy: Radical Simplicity

Our core user is a non-technical, time-starved community leader. That's why ease of use is our most important feature. We believe powerful software can and should be intuitive. Every screen, button, and workflow is designed to reduce complexity and give you back time, so you can focus on what matters most.

Our Commitment: Win Omaha First

Our go-to-market strategy is hyper-focused on our home. We are committed to building deep relationships with local partners like the Nonprofit Association of the Midlands and the Omaha Community Foundation. By cultivating a cohort of local "Founding Members," we can build a powerful base of trust, credibility, and success stories before expanding further. This isn't just a business plan; it's our commitment to our community.
